PizzA




Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (or chicken/veggie patty)

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

  • 4 burger buns

  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles

  • Cheese slices (optional)

  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayo (optional)




Instructions:

  1. Prepare the patties: Season the ground meat with salt and pepper. Form into patties about the size of your buns.
  2. Cook the patties: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 3-5 minutes per side or until cooked through. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking if desired.
  3. Assemble the burger: Toast the burger buns lightly. Place the patty on the bottom bun, and add toppings like lettuce, tomato, pickles, and condiments. Top with the other bun.
