BeeF with GingER




Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef (sliced thinly)

  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger (julienned)

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce

  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar

  • 1/2 onion (sliced)

  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)

  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

  • Salt and pepper to taste




Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry beef until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add ginger, onion, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir to combine.
  4. Return beef to the pan, toss everything together, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with rice or noodles!
