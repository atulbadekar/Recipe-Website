DeseRT




Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp flour

  • 4 tbsp sugar

  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract




Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together all dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute 30 seconds or until fully cooked.
  4. Let it cool slightly and enjoy your warm, chocolatey treat!
