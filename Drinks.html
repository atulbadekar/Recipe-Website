DrinK's




Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice (about 4-5 lemons)

  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)

  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes

  • Lemon slices (for garnish)




Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, mix lemon juice and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add cold water and stir well.
  3. Serve over ice and garnish with lemon slices.
  4. Enjoy your refreshing, homemade lemonade!
